Release

Dawn FM is the fifth studio album by Canadian singer-songwriter The Weeknd that was released on the 7th of January, 2022 by XO and Republic Records. The album was preceded by the lead single Take My Breath, which peaked at number six on the US Billboard Hot 100. Intended to serve as a direct follow-up to his fourth studio album After Hours, Dawn FM features guest appearances from Jim Carrey, Tyler, the Creator and Lil Wayne.
